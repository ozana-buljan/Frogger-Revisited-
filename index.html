How to Play

Goal is to collect as much points as you can!

Getting points

Each time frog reaches the water across the street without being struck by an enemy you get 10 points!

Each time frog collects a fly, you get 10 points!

Each time frog collects a dragonfly, 20 points

Each time frog collects a butterfly, 30 points!

Each time frog collects a heart, you get an extra life plus 5 points!

Moving around

Press on your keyboard to move around

Be wary of the obstacles! You shall not pass them!

Level UP

Your progress is being tracked trough levels. The more more points you collect - heigher you go!

Good luck!
